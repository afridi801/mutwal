About Our Firm

20+ Years of Legal Excellence in Karachi

Founded by Matwali Afridi in 2003, Afridi Law Associates has grown from a solo practice to one of Karachi's most respected law firms. Our founder's vision was to create a practice that combines traditional legal values with innovative strategies to serve clients effectively in Pakistan's complex legal environment.

Mr. Afridi began his career as a public prosecutor before establishing his private practice, giving him unique insights into both sides of the legal system. This experience informs our approach to every case we handle.

Why Choose Our Firm:

  • Strategic approach tailored to each client's specific needs
  • Proven track record of successful outcomes
  • Commitment to integrity and ethical practice
